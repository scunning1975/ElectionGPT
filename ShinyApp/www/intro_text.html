WELCOME TO ElectionGPT

An interactive open-source software tool for virtual analysis of 2024 presidential prediction using ChatGPT training data and news articles.

ElectionGPT enables intuitive, rapid, and reproducible quality of prediction analysis at both the state level and the national level. It facilitates understanding and communication of prediction trends and model performances.

 WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH ELECTIONGPT

  • Compare the winning probabilities of Harris and Trump across states, based on four narrative voices (Direct Reporter, Fox News, MSNBC, BBC).
  • Track winning trends over time for each state. Adjust the analysis period to see how predictions change daily. 
  • View national prediction trends, showing daily simulations of Harris and Trump securing the electoral majority.
