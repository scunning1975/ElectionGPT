In contrast, Trump has positioned himself as the candidate of law and order, promising to tackle the challenges at the southern border and criticizing Harris's policies as contributing to crime and chaos. Conversely, Trump has garnered backing from influential figures in the conservative sphere, including the National Border Patrol Council, which solidifies his appeal among law enforcement supporters.
