The election saw Trump winning a total of 304 electoral votes to Clinton's 227, despite Clinton securing nearly 2 million more popular votes nationwide. Clinton, in her concession speech, stated, “This is painful, and it will be for a long time. But I want you to remember that our campaign was about a vision for America.” Her acknowledgement of the electoral defeat came amidst a backdrop of uncertainty regarding the future of the Democratic Party.
